Find us

Lost?

We are located on Queens Road in Hethersett

If Travelling from Wymondham...

  • Take Harts Farm Road along the B1172
  • At the roundabout, turn left
  • At the next roundabout by Waitrose, turn right onto the B1172
  • Follow the B1172 for 2.1 miles
  • After passing Hethersett Old Hall School on the right, take the next exit onto Norwich Road
  • Follow Norwich Road round past the Kings' Head and then the Queens' Head pubs
  • Turn left onto Queen's Road
  • Follow Queen's Road
  • Find us on the right, by Hethersett Library

If Travelling from Norwich

  • Follow the A11 (Newmarket Road) from Norwich into Eaton
  • At the first roundabout, continue straight on
  • At the second roundabout, take the third exit onto the B1172 next to the Thickthorn Bus Station
  • Follow the B1172, turning left on the first roundabout
  • Continue following the B1172 for 1.5 miles
  • After passing the St. Remigius Church on your left, take the next right onto Norwich Road.
  • Take the second right onto Queen's Road
  • Follow Queen's Road
  • Find us on the right, by Hethersett Library
