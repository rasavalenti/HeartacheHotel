Services and Facilities

On-Site Gym

Step into our on-site 24-hour gym, featuring a cardiovascular area with treadmills and cycling machines, plus a free weight room and an abundance of high quality gym equipment. No subscriptions required. No children below age 16.

Breakfast Buffet

Breakfast is served from 6am until 10am every day for all customers. We provide full-English and continental options. Vegan, gluten-free, and lactose-intolerant options are also available. Please consult a member of staff if you suffer from any allergies.

Meeting Rooms

We have three meeting rooms of varying capacity available for business parties, subject to booking and availability. All rooms are provided with presentation facilities. Business parties must be able to provide company details upon booking.
